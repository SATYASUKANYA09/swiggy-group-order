# User Personas: Swiggy Group Ordering & Split Bill Feature

---

## Persona 1 — Riya Sharma, 22, College Student

**Location:** Hyderabad | **Occupation:** B.Tech final year student

### A Day in Her Life
Riya lives in a hostel with 5 friends. Every evening they decide to order
food together on Swiggy. She always ends up placing the order for everyone,
pays the full amount, and then spends the next 2 days reminding people to
pay her back on UPI.

### Primary Goal
Order food with friends without being the one who pays for everything and
chases people for money.

### Top 3 Frustrations
- Always has to be the "middleman" who collects everyone's orders manually
- Friends forget to pay her back, making it awkward
- Can't easily split delivery charges fairly

### What Would Make Her Love This Feature
If each friend could add their own items and pay for themselves directly —
she never has to collect money again.

### Device & Tech Comfort
- Mobile only (Android)
- Tech comfort: 8/10 — uses UPI, Swiggy, Instagram daily

### Her Quote
> "I love ordering with friends but I hate being the one who always has
> to remind everyone to pay me back."

---

## Persona 2 — Arjun Mehta, 28, Software Engineer

**Location:** Bengaluru | **Occupation:** SDE at a mid-size startup

### A Day in Her Life
Arjun orders lunch with his team of 6 people every weekday. Someone
creates a WhatsApp poll, everyone messages their orders separately,
one person places the combined order, and then there's a whole
conversation about who owes what. It wastes 20 minutes every day.

### Primary Goal
Place a single group order where everyone picks their own food and
pays their own share — without any WhatsApp coordination.

### Top 3 Frustrations
- The whole process of collecting orders over WhatsApp is chaotic
- Someone always changes their mind after the order is placed
- Payment collection after delivery is always messy

### What Would Make Him Love This Feature
A shareable link where teammates can add their own items within a
time limit — and everyone pays at checkout individually.

### Device & Tech Comfort
- Mobile + desktop
- Tech comfort: 9/10 — power user, uses Swiggy almost daily

### His Quote
> "We waste so much time just figuring out who wants what and who
> owes whom. There has to be a better way."

---

## Key Insight from Both Personas
Both Riya and Arjun are frequent Swiggy users who already order in
groups regularly — the problem isn't getting them to use the feature,
it's removing the coordination friction that currently happens
outside the app (WhatsApp + UPI). The feature needs to bring that
entire workflow inside Swiggy.
