## Now (MVP — Build First)
- Shareable group order link
- Individual cart per member
- Individual payment at checkout
- Real-time cart view for organiser
- Time limit setting

## Next (3-6 Months)
- Split delivery charge equally among members
- Nudge notification when time limit is almost up

## Later (Future)
- In-app chat between group members
- Multi-address delivery
- Past group order history

---

## Success Metrics (After 3 Months)
- 15% of group orders (3+ people) use the feature
- Average group size: 3.5 members per session
- Feature satisfaction rating: 4.2/5
- Drop-off at "join session" step: under 20%
